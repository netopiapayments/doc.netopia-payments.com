---

To enable online payments on your online store, follow these steps:

1. [Create a new account](/docs/get-started/signup) on netopia-payments.com and verify your phone number and email address.
2. Complete the ["Know Your Customer"](/docs/get-started/kyc) (KYC) process, during which we'll collect the necessary information to activate your account in compliance with banking regulations.
3. Set up and activate an online [point of sale](/docs/get-started/point-of-sale), ensuring that all required conditions are met.
4. Implement the NETOPIA Payments API by following the provided [documentation](/docs/get-started/technical-implementation).

Once these steps are completed, your shop will be ready to accept online payments.
